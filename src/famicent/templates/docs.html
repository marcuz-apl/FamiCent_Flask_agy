{# Welcome Section #}
Welcome to FamiCent

FamiCent is a secure, local family finance tracker designed to help your household keep tabs on utility bills, credit card limits, subscriptions, and custom accounts in one central place. It runs entirely on your local machine, ensuring complete privacy with zero cloud dependencies.

Important Rule

Because FamiCent uses local database encryption keyed by your master password, losing your password means your data cannot be recovered. Keep your password safe!

{# Account & Payment Guides #}
Managing Accounts & Payments
  • 1. Create Accounts

    Navigate to the Accounts section and click + Add Account. You can record balances, interest rates, and custom portal login details.

  • 2. Track Payments

    Go to Payments to log card payments, transfers, or cash receipts against any account. This updates your monthly metrics automatically.

{# Security & MFA Guides #}
Security & MFA Guide
  • TOTP Multi-Factor Authentication

    MFA is mandatory. During setup, scan the QR code using Google Authenticator, Authy, or any standard authenticator app to retrieve your 6-digit TOTP codes.

  • Inactivity Auto-Lock

    FamiCent features a strict 5-minute inactivity session lock. You can customize this session duration limit under Settings.

{# Database & Role Guidelines #}
Administrator Tools

If you are logged in as an Administrator, you have access to two specialized tools in the sidebar:

  • 👥 Users Management: Admins can add up to 5 family member profiles (Editors or Viewers), reset credentials, reset MFA enrollment, or adjust user-specific session lock timers.
  • 🔎 DB Looker (Viewer): Run custom, read-only SQL queries to directly inspect database structure and records (write commands are strictly blocked for security).
